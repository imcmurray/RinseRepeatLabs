## Flexible Funding Models

We understand that every project and every business has unique financial circumstances. That's why we offer flexible funding options to match your needs.

### Option 1: Full Upfront Payment
**You keep 100% of revenue**

Pay the full development cost upfront and retain complete ownership of all revenue generated by your product.

**Best for:**
- Established businesses with available capital
- Projects with proven market demand
- Clients who want complete financial independence

### Option 2: Revenue Share (70/30)
**Reduced upfront cost**

Pay a significantly reduced development fee, and we take a 30% share of ongoing revenue. You receive transparent monthly reports and 70% of all earnings.

**Best for:**
- Startups conserving runway
- First-time founders testing ideas
- Projects with subscription or recurring revenue

### Option 3: Revenue Share (50/50)
**Minimal or zero upfront cost**

Pay little to no upfront fee, and we share revenue equally. We're fully invested in your success because our earnings depend on it.

**Best for:**
- Early-stage startups
- Innovative ideas with high potential
- Founders without initial capital

### How Revenue Sharing Works

1. **App Store Payouts**: We handle the App Store/Play Store payout setup
2. **Monthly Reports**: Receive detailed revenue and download metrics
3. **Direct Payments**: Your share is transferred directly to you each month
4. **Full Transparency**: Access to all financial data and analytics
