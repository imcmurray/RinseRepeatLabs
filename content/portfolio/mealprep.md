## Overview

MealPrep Pro is a comprehensive meal planning application that helps families save time and eat healthier. The app combines recipe discovery, meal planning, automated grocery lists, and nutrition tracking in one seamless experience.

## The Challenge

MealPrep Solutions came to us with the goal of creating an app that would make healthy eating accessible for busy families. The challenges included:

- Creating an intuitive planning experience for all skill levels
- Building a recipe recommendation engine that considers dietary restrictions
- Automating grocery list creation with store-specific optimization
- Implementing family sharing with individual preferences

## Our Solution

We developed MealPrep Pro for mobile (React Native) and web (React), with a shared component library for consistency. The app uses AI to provide personalized recipe recommendations.

### Key Features Developed

**Smart Recipe Suggestions**
AI-powered recommendations based on dietary preferences, past meals, and what's in your pantry.

**Drag-and-Drop Meal Planner**
Visual weekly calendar for planning meals with the whole family.

**Automatic Grocery Lists**
One-tap list generation organized by store sections, with quantity adjustment for family size.

**Pantry Tracking**
Keep track of what you have to reduce waste and inspire meals.

**Nutrition Dashboard**
Track nutritional intake across the family with personalized goals.

**Family Sharing**
Multiple profiles with individual dietary restrictions and preferences.
