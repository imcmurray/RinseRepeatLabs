## Overview

MomentForge helps users discover and execute personalized experiences—from spontaneous local outings to full weekend trips—using location data, preferences, and real-time context to make every day more meaningful.

## The Challenge

People crave memorable experiences but often lack time or inspiration to plan them. MomentForge needed to solve this with proactive, accurate recommendations. Key challenges included:

- Generating context-aware itineraries in seconds
- Integrating calendars, weather, events, and local data reliably
- Automating post-experience memory capture without manual input
- Balancing deep personalization with strong privacy protections

## Our Solution

We used Flutter for smooth, beautiful cross-platform performance and rich maps/visuals. AI drives proactive planning while automatic highlight generation turns experiences into lasting memories.

### Key Features Developed

**Instant Itinerary Builder**
Input mood, budget, energy, and interests for fully tailored plans in seconds.

**Real-Time Adjustments**
Dynamically adapts to weather changes, traffic, or spontaneous pivots.

**Auto-Memory Creation**
Generates polished photo books, journals, and shareable stories from your captured moments.

**Surprise Mode**
Delivers low-effort, high-fun spontaneous adventures curated just for you.

## Results

- **120,000+** users in the launch year
- **4.8 star** average rating
- **70%** of users report more frequent memorable experiences
- Highlighted in travel and lifestyle app recommendations
