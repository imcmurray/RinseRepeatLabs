## Overview

FeverTap is a rhythm-tap mobile puzzle game where players tap colorful orbs before they fade. Chain same-colored orbs for combo multipliers and enter Fever Mode for bonus points. With a dark, moody aesthetic and neon purple accents, the game delivers an immersive experience across multiple game modes.

## Orb Types

FeverTap features 12 unique orb mechanics that keep gameplay fresh and challenging:

**Core Orbs**
- **Standard**: Tap before it fades
- **Hold-to-Capture**: Press and hold to capture
- **Drifting**: Moves across the screen
- **Shrinking**: Gets smaller over time

**Advanced Orbs**
- **Pulsing**: Tap on the beat
- **Shield**: Requires multiple taps
- **Bouncing**: Rebounds off screen edges
- **Ghost**: Fades in and out of visibility

**Special Orbs**
- **Bomb**: Tap quickly or lose points
- **Split**: Divides into smaller orbs
- **Sequence**: Must be tapped in order
- **Decoy**: Avoid these imposters
