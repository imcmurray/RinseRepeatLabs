## Overview

FeverTap ("the App") is a mobile game developed by Rinse Repeat Labs. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it's used, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### Local Game Data
All game data is stored locally on your device only:
- Game progress and scores
- Settings and preferences
- Unlocked content and achievements
- Journal entries and vignettes

**This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.**

### No Personal Information
The App does not collect, store, or transmit:
- Names, email addresses, or contact information
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking purposes
- Photos, files, or other personal content

### No Analytics or Tracking
The App does not use analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

## In-App Purchases

If you make in-app purchases:
- All payment processing is handled by Google Play (Android)
- We do not receive or store your payment information
- Google's privacy policy governs payment data handling
- Purchase records are stored locally on your device

For Google Play's privacy practices, see: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally using secure on-device storage
- Data is protected by your device's built-in security
- Uninstalling the App removes all associated data

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

FeverTap is designed to be suitable for all ages:
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
- No account creation or personal data entry is required
- No social features, chat, or user-generated content sharing
- No behavioral advertising or targeted content

Parents can feel confident that their children's privacy is protected while using this App.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data, except:
- **Google Play Services** (Android): Required for app installation and in-app purchases

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access your data**: View all game data directly in the App
- **Delete your data**: Clear progress in Settings, or uninstall the App
- **Control notifications**: Enable/disable in device settings

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated through App updates.
